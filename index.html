About My Journey

Blending academics with the creative impulse.

The Artist's Heart

Painting is my form of meditation and expression. Whether it's the vibrant hues of a Kerala sunset or a complex portrait, my passion lies in bringing emotions to life on canvas. I mainly focus on traditional mediums like acrylics and watercolors.

Student & Cultural Roots

As a student, I value structure and learning, which complements my creative pursuits. My home state of **Kerala**—with its rich colors, dense nature, and unique traditions—is a constant source of inspiration for my art.
